About Engineering Physics

Engineering Physics at UBC is all about approaching the different aspects of electrical, mechanical, and software engineering from a physics first approach. While this means it contains a wide breadth of material, the strong analytical math and physics core keeps it focused on bottom up problem-solving.

About my specialization

While the focus of my specialization is physics and electronics, I am familiar, and enjoy working with many other fields of engineering. From circuit analysis to statistical mechanics, Engineering Physics gives me a strong grasp on fundamentals, and how to better integrate my contribution into them.
